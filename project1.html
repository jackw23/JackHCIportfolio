Project 1: Campus Improvements

During this project, my group and I were tasked with coming up with a solution to an existing human-computer interaction problem here at HKUST. We started first by brainstorming what we wanted out topic to be. Drawing on our own personal experience, we decided to tackle the issues of eating HKUST canteens. Our group brainstormed a variety of questions that we would send out to HKUST students to identify what their pain points were and thus how we could solve them. Coming up with good questions to ask people is rather difficult as you have to make sure they are easy to understand and unbiased. Our group made mulitple tweaks to the final set of questions before we released them. Next, we had to analysis the data from the questions. We discovered 4 major pain points: lack of seating, lack of ingredient/allergen transparency, long wait times, and inefficient in-person ordering. Analyzing the data actually was not too hard since nearly all the responses fell into these 4 categories. From these findings, we started to develop solutions in the form of storyboards.
